Terms of Use

By using SafeWaste AI, you agree to follow the rules and guidelines below. This system is designed for hospital waste classification and educational purposes.

System Purpose

SafeWaste AI is an AI-based tool used to classify biomedical waste into categories like infectious, sharps, pharmaceutical, and general waste. It is intended to assist healthcare staff, not replace human judgment.

Important Disclaimer

This system does NOT provide medical diagnosis or treatment advice. AI predictions are based on trained datasets and may not always be 100% accurate. Final decisions must always be made by trained healthcare professionals.
