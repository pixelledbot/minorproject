Biomedical Waste Management Overview

Biomedical waste is any waste generated during diagnosis, treatment, or immunization of humans or animals. It is highly hazardous because it can contain infectious microorganisms, toxic chemicals, or sharp objects.

Main Categories
  • Infectious Waste
  • Sharps Waste
  • Pharmaceutical Waste
  • General Waste
Why Proper Management is Important

Improper handling can spread diseases like HIV, Hepatitis B & C, and cause environmental contamination. Proper segregation reduces infection risk and ensures safe disposal.
